My Photos

Photography has been a hobby of mine for quite a while. I enjoy taking photos of nature, cityscapes, and my friends and family. Many of my photos are taken during my travels or my everyday life. I enjoy both digital and film photography, and have recently begun developing my own film in the darkroom, which I find gives me an exciting new sense of control over my photos.
